OUR STORY

We are Miniclip

An international digital games and entertainment company. A leader in multiplayer games, with a purpose to ‘unleash the gamer in everyone’ with more than 7 billion downloads to date. Powered by amazing people.

What we do

We develop, publish and distribute multiplayer mobile games. We reach over 400 million players each month, and our audience keeps growing. Our experience, passion, and people provide us with a unique understanding of the digital games space, with extensive worldwide distribution and an internationally recognised brand.
